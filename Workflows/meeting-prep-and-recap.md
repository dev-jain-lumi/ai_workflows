# Meeting Prep and Recap Workflow

## Trigger

- Prepare for this meeting
- Convert meeting notes to actions

## Required Input

- meeting context
- attendees
- agenda
- notes (if recap)

## Steps Before Meeting

1. Define objective and desired outcome.
2. List decision points and sensitive topics.
3. Draft key questions by stakeholder.
4. Prepare ask and fallback position.

## Steps After Meeting

1. Extract decisions made.
2. Convert notes into concrete actions.
3. Assign owners and due dates.
4. Capture open questions and risks.
5. Draft follow-up message.

## Output

Before:

- objective
- desired outcome
- questions to ask
- sensitive topics
- decisions needed

After:

- decisions
- actions
- owners
- open questions
- risks
- follow-up message

## Save Output

- Artifacts/meeting-notes/YYYY-MM-DD-meeting-topic.md
- Use Templates/meeting-recap.md
