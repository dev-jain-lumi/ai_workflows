# Management Principles

## Decision Principles

1. Prefer clarity over completeness.
2. Name one decision owner for every decision.
3. Separate reversible and irreversible decisions.
4. Time-box analysis to the decision impact.
5. Record assumptions and review triggers.

## Delivery Principles

1. Surface risks early, with owner and mitigation.
2. Escalate blockers with explicit asks.
3. Keep WIP limited and priorities visible.
4. Tie delivery updates to outcomes, not activity.

## Communication Principles

1. Executive communication must be understandable in 60 seconds.
2. Put ask and risk before background detail.
3. Distinguish facts, assumptions, and opinions.
4. Use plain language and concrete next actions.

## Governance Principles

1. No artifact without owner and review date.
2. No action without owner and checkpoint.
3. No major decision without alternatives considered.
4. Archive superseded artifacts to preserve traceability.
